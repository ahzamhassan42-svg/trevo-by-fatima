📲 How to Order

Ordering from Trevo is simple and fast! Follow these easy steps to place your order via WhatsApp:

  • Browse Products
    Explore our collection on the Products page. Use the search bar or category filters to find exactly what you need.
  • Add to Cart
    Click the "+ Add to Cart" button on any product you'd like to purchase. You can add multiple items.
  • Review Your Cart
    Go to the Cart page to review your items. Adjust quantities or remove products as needed.
  • Choose Delivery Method
    Select between Standard Delivery (5–7 working days, Rs. 199) or Urgent Delivery (2–3 working days, Rs. 350).
  • Checkout via WhatsApp
    Click the "Checkout via WhatsApp" button. Your order summary will be sent automatically to our WhatsApp.
  • Confirm & Receive
    We'll confirm your order on WhatsApp, share tracking details, and deliver to your doorstep. Pay via Cash on Delivery!

🔒 Privacy Policy

At Trevo, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Your name, phone number, and delivery address — shared via WhatsApp when you place an order.
  • Order Details: Products you purchase, quantities, and payment method.
  • Device Information: Basic browser data and cookies to improve your shopping experience on our website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate with you about your purchases, delivery updates, and customer support.
  • To improve our products, website, and overall customer experience.
  • To send you occasional updates about new products or offers (only if you opt in).

Data Protection

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Your data is only shared with our trusted courier partners for the sole purpose of delivering your orders.
  • We use secure communication channels (WhatsApp end-to-end encryption) for all order-related conversations.

Cookies

Our website uses local storage to save your shopping cart between visits. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics are used.

Your Rights
